Our Services

What We Provide

  • Fillings & Orthodontics

    Our dental experts will check your teeth, gums, and mouth for issues, using advanced tools for early detection.

  • Dental Exams

    Our dental experts will check your teeth, gums, and mouth for issues, using advanced tools for early detection.

  • Teeth Cleaning

    Our thorough cleanings remove plaque and tartar, preventing cavities and gum disease.

  • Crowns and Bridges

    Crowns and bridges restore the function and appearance of damaged or missing teeth.

  • Extractions

    Safe and efficient tooth extractions for damaged or problematic teeth. Our expert care ensures minimal discomfort and promotes quick healing.

  • Dentures and Partials

    Dentures and partials provide effective solutions for missing teeth. Regain your smile and confidence with our high-quality dentures and partials.

  • TMD

    Relieve TMD pain and anxiety with our specialized care. We offer effective treatments for temporomandibular disorders and provide sedation options for a relaxed, stress-free dental experience.

  • Sedation Dentistry

    Sedation dentistry uses medication to help patients relax during dental procedures. It's ideal for those with anxiety, low pain tolerance, or a strong gag reflex.

  • ⁠Root Canal Treatments

    Root canal treatments involve removing the infected or damaged pulp from inside a tooth, cleaning and disinfecting the area, and then filling and sealing it.

  • Smile Designs

    Smile Designs offers advanced, personalized dental care, including preventive, cosmetic, and restorative services. Our expert team ensures optimal oral health and beautiful smiles.

  • Implants

    Dental implants are artificial tooth roots made of titanium that are surgically placed into the jawbone. They provide a permanent base for fixed or removable replacement teeth
